My Learning Journey

I am an aspiring educator from the beautiful village of Yabrang in Trashigang Dzongkhag, Bhutan. Rooted in the rich culture and traditions of my homeland, I am a dedicated learner and future teacher who dreams of using technology to transform education in my community. I believe in the power of knowledge and continuous learning to create positive change. With a strong passion for ICT and teaching, I am committed to guiding students to become innovative thinkers, lifelong learners, and responsible digital citizens. My journey is driven by the desire to bridge the gap between tradition and technology, making learning accessible and meaningful for all.

Born and raised in the beautiful Bhutan. When I am not busy learning, you will likely find me exploring new technologies, reading educational content, or sharing knowledge with my friends.

EDUCATION

An overview of my academic background, from early schooling to tertiary education.

2009-2015
Yabrang Primary School

In my world as a primary student, life is all about making new friends and discovering everything. My days are filled with learning how to read and write, drawing colorful pictures, and playing games outside with my classmates. It is a fun mix of being in a classroom and getting to explore and create with my own hands.

2016-2019
Dungtse Central School

As a middle school student, my world is getting bigger and more complex. Instead of one teacher, I have a different teacher for each subject, and I have to switch classes throughout the day. I have more homework and bigger projects, and I'm learning how to manage my time. The social scene is also a lot more important, and I am figuring out where I fit in while trying to balance my studies with my friends.

2020-2022
Rangjung Higher School

As a higher school student, my world is all about preparing for the future. The classes are more challenging, with a bigger focus on exams and choosing subjects that will lead to a career. I have more freedom to choose what I study, and I'm also managing a busier schedule with clubs and social events. It's a time of figuring out my own interests and taking more responsibility for my studies and my life.

2023-Present
Samtse College of Education

As a B.Ed. ICT student at Samtse College of Education, my experience is much more focused than in previous schools. My days are a mix of studying educational theories and getting hands-on with technology. I'm not just learning to code or use software; I'm learning how to teach it effectively to future students.
